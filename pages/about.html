▶ About Us

Your Free Streaming
Destination

RongKunKmeanMnus is a free video streaming platform bringing together the best Chinese Drama, Thai Drama, and entertainment videos — curated, organized, and always free to watch.

Who We Are

RongKunKmeanMnus is an independent video curation platform based in Cambodia. We hand-pick and organize video content from trusted public platforms like Dailymotion and Rumble, making it easy for our audience to discover great content in one place.

We are not a video hosting service. We embed publicly available videos and organize them into categories and playlists to create a better viewing experience for our users.

What We Offer

🎬 Curated Video Collections
Hand-picked videos organized into playlists and categories. We focus on Chinese Drama, Thai Drama, and popular entertainment series dubbed in Khmer.
📋 Series Playlists
Watch full drama series in order from Episode 1 to the latest episode. New episodes are added regularly.
🔍 Easy Search & Discovery
Find any video or series instantly by title, category, or keyword. Browse by genre or use the search bar.
📱 Free on Any Device
Fully responsive — watch comfortably on your phone, tablet, or desktop. No account needed, always free.
📖 Watch History
Your watch history is saved locally on your device so you can always continue where you left off.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make great video content — especially Asian drama series — easy to find and enjoyable to watch for Cambodian audiences. We believe everyone deserves access to quality entertainment without paywalls or complicated sign-ups.

Content Sources

All videos on RongKunKmeanMnus are embedded from Dailymotion and Rumble using their official embed APIs. We do not host, upload, download, or store any video files on our servers. All video rights belong to their respective creators and copyright holders.

If you are a content creator or rights holder and wish to have your content removed, please contact us and we will act promptly.

Advertising

RongKunKmeanMnus is supported by advertising through Google AdSense. Ads help us keep the platform free for all users. We do not allow advertisers to influence our content curation decisions. For more information see our Privacy Policy.
