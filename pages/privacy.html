This Privacy Policy explains how RongKunKmeanMnus collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website.

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may automatically collect the following information when you visit our site:

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, time spent, browser type, device type, and operating system.
  • IP Address: Used for analytics and security purposes only.
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device used by Google AdSense and analytics services.

We also collect information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, and message when you use our Contact form.

2. Watch History

Your watch history is stored locally on your device only using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers. You can clear your watch history at any time from the History page on our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.
